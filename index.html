Ever wondered how they became so popular ?

Ah Netflix! A service many of you probably use at least once a day, whether it is to watch that newly released episode of The Crown, or to rewatch Friends for the hundredth time.

Unfortunately it’s a weekday’s evening, and you ran out of things to watch. You start scrolling through their app to find a binge-worthy TV show or a dramatic movie, and then a thought crosses your mind.

What’s their strategy when releasing new content? Do they target a specific audience? How international is their catalogue?

Before going into deeper thoughts, let’s find out!

Who's watching ?

Think twice before endlessly scrolling down and hoping to find what you’re looking for, Netflix’s catalogue is enormous. Next thing you know it’ll be time for bed, we warned you!

If you’ve ever wondered why there are much more movies than TV shows, don’t forget the streaming service started out as an online DVD-rental in the United States.

Chances are you probably do not live there but still can access the platform. That’s because in 2010, they started expanding their services to different regions of the world. Are all parts of the world watching the same anglophone content? Definitely not.
